    A Train Ride

    Myanmar from Cameron Parr

    A beautiful video captured by a tourist. It highlights the beautiful people of myanmar and some of the pristine rural villages. You will get to see the country side if you take a train ride from Yangon to Mandalay. Trains are rather slow. So, make sure you have plenty of time on the road, on the train. But it'll be worth it.

    Arial Bagan

    Visualtraveling - Myanmar from Patrik Wallner

    Another high quality footage of Myanmar. The video shows awesome arial coverage of Bagan -- the ancient, historic city located in central Myanmar. When I visited Bagan for the first time, I was surprised by the sheer volume and density of pagodas that occupied Bagan land. It shows a high civilization and Bagan was certainly ahead of its time.
